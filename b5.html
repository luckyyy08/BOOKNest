Chhava, written by renowned author Shivaji Sawant, is a powerful historical novel that narrates the life of Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj, the brave son of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. The book brings alive Sambhaji’s extraordinary courage, intelligence, and leadership in defending the Maratha empire against the Mughal onslaught. It portrays his struggles, sacrifices, and the immense challenges he faced as a warrior and a king. More than just history, Chhava reflects the emotional depth of Sambhaji’s life — his determination, his human vulnerabilities, and his undying loyalty to Swarajya. Considered a classic of Marathi literature, it continues to inspire readers with the tale of one of India’s greatest warrior kings.
