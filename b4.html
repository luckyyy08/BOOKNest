Shriman Yogi, Ranjit Desai’s masterpiece first published in 1968, is a celebrated biographical novel on the life of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj — the most revered and inspirational figure in Maharashtra. Based on deep historical research, the novel traces Shivaji’s journey from birth to his rise as a visionary king. Desai presents Shivaji not as a mythical figure, but as a human with unmatched courage, compassion, and an unshakable love for his motherland. Through meticulous storytelling, Shriman Yogi highlights Shivaji’s qualities as a fearless leader, a just ruler, and a nobleman who respected all religions. The novel immerses readers so deeply that by its end, one feels the personal loss of Shivaji himself. A literary classic, Shriman Yogi remains a timeless tribute to the Maratha king’s life, struggles, and legacy.
